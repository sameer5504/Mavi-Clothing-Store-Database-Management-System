# Mavi Clothing Store Database Management System

A full-stack database-driven web application designed to manage sales, inventory, and supplier operations for **Mavi Palestine**, a clothing retail business.

---

## 📖 Overview

This project was developed as part of the **Database Systems (COMP 333)** course at Birzeit University.

The system replaces manual and spreadsheet-based tracking with a centralized database solution that improves:
- Inventory tracking
- Sales recording
- Supplier management
- Reporting and decision-making

The system connects the **warehouse and retail store** to ensure accurate and real-time data flow.

## 🚀 Features

### 🛍️ Sales Management
- Record customer purchases
- Store transaction history
- Calculate totals dynamically

### 📦 Inventory & Warehouse Management
- Track product quantities in store and warehouse
- Automatic stock updates after sales and transfers
- Monitor low-stock items

### 📥 Purchase & Supplier Management
- Create and manage purchase orders
- Track supplier information
- Support partial payments

### 🔄 Stock Transfers
- Transfer products between warehouse and store
- Maintain full transfer history

### 👥 Employee & Customer Management
- Manage employee roles and authentication
- Store customer purchase data

---

## 🧠 Database Design

The system is built using a relational database model with key entities such as:

- Product
- Supplier
- Inventory
- Warehouse
- Store
- Employee
- Customer
- Sale & Sale Details
- Purchase & Purchase Details
- Stock Transfer & Payment

✔ Fully normalized to **3NF (Third Normal Form)**  
✔ Eliminates redundancy and ensures data consistency :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
